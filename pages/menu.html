Hot Coffee

Ingredients Instant coffee - 1/2 tbsp Sugar - 1 tsp or as needed Hot water - 1 tbsp Hot milk - 250 ml (I microwaved the milk but you can also heat it on the stovetop) Tips Any type of milk would work for this recipe. I used about 150ml of semi-skimmed milk and 100ml water.
